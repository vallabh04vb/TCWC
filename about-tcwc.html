About TCWC

The Clean Water Challenge

An initiative by Bharat Clean Rivers Foundation in partnership with The Ocean Cleanup, empowering students to take action against plastic pollution and champion a Blue Economy. Mumbai’s young changemakers are invited to be part of this movement.

The Challenge
Students are invited to express their vision for a cleaner future, turning creativity into awareness and action through Fine Arts, Poetry and Essay Writing.

Our Impact

Community Engagement

Bringing together students, educators, and environmental advocates to work toward a common goal.

Awareness Creation

Using art and writing to spread awareness about water pollution and conservation.

Action Inspiration

Motivating young people to take concrete steps toward environmental protection.

Policy Influence

Creating a movement that can influence environmental policy and corporate practices.

Competition Categories

Fine Art

Express your vision for clean waters through painting, drawing, or sculpture.

  • Open to students aged 12-22
  • Artwork must be original
  • Theme: "A Blue Future"

Literary Art

Create poetry, short stories, or plays that highlight water conservation.

  • Open to students aged 12-22
  • Word limit: 1,500 words
  • Can be in English, Hindi, or Marathi

Essay Writing

Research and write about solutions to plastic pollution in our waters.

  • Open to students aged 15-22
  • Word limit: 2,000 words
  • Must include research and citations
