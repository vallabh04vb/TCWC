Guidelines & FAQ's

Competition Guidelines

The Clean Waters Challenge invites students to express their vision for a cleaner future through art and creative writing. Below are the guidelines for participation in the competition.

Download Complete Guidelines

Eligibility

  • Open to students aged 12-22 years
  • Must be enrolled in a school or college in Mumbai
  • Individual participation only (no group submissions)

Fine Art Category

  • Painting, drawing, or sculpture
  • Maximum size: 24" x 36" for 2D works
  • Digital art must be submitted as high-resolution prints
  • Theme: "A Blue Future"

Literary Art Category

  • Poetry, short stories, or plays
  • Word limit: 1,500 words
  • Can be in English, Hindi, or Marathi
  • Theme: "Voices of Our Rivers"

Essay Writing Category

  • Research-based essays
  • Word limit: 2,000 words
  • Must include citations and references
  • Theme: "Solutions for Plastic Pollution"

Important Dates

  • Registration Opens: February 15, 2023
  • Registration Closes: March 30, 2023
  • Submission Deadline: April 15, 2023
  • Winners Announced: June 5, 2023

Prizes

  • First Prize: ₹25,000 in each category
  • Second Prize: ₹15,000 in each category
  • Third Prize: ₹10,000 in each category
  • Special Recognition Awards: ₹5,000 each

Submission Process

1

Register

Complete the online registration form with your personal and school/college details.

2

Create

Develop your artwork or written piece according to the guidelines for your chosen category.

3

Submit

Upload your completed work through the submission portal that will be shared with registered participants.

4

Judging

Entries will be evaluated by a panel of experts in art, literature, and environmental science.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in The Clean Waters Challenge?

The competition is open to students aged 12-22 years who are enrolled in schools or colleges in Mumbai. Participants must register individually, as group submissions are not accepted.

Can I submit entries in multiple categories?

Yes, you can participate in multiple categories. However, you must register separately for each category and submit distinct works for each.

Is there a registration fee?

No, participation in The Clean Waters Challenge is completely free of charge.

How will the winners be selected?

Entries will be judged based on creativity, relevance to the theme, technical skill, and the impact of the message. A panel of experts in art, literature, and environmental science will evaluate all submissions.

Will my work be displayed publicly?

Yes, selected entries will be featured in a public exhibition and on the TCWC website. By participating, you grant permission for your work to be displayed with proper attribution.

Can I submit work that has been previously published or displayed?

No, all submissions must be original and created specifically for The Clean Waters Challenge. Previously published or displayed work is not eligible.

How will I know if I've won?

Winners will be announced on June 5, 2023 (World Environment Day) on the TCWC website and social media channels. Winners will also be notified directly via email.
